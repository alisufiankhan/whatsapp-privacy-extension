Features

Built for real-world privacy

Every feature designed for the moments that matter. Open offices, classrooms, public transport.

01

Blur messages

Text stays hidden until you hover. No accidental reveals, no anxiety.

02

Hide media

Photos and videos blur automatically. You decide when to view them.

03

Anonymous contacts

Names and profile pictures blurred. Your social network stays private.

04

Instant toggle

One keyboard shortcut flips everything on or off. Zero delay, zero animation.

05

Typing blur

Your draft messages stay hidden too. Nobody reads what you're composing.

06

No performance hit

Lightweight by design. WhatsApp runs exactly as fast as before.

Running in under 60 seconds

Four steps. No account. No configuration screens.

01

Install

One click from the Chrome Web Store. No sign-up required.

02

Open WhatsApp

Navigate to web.whatsapp.com. The extension activates automatically.

03

Configure

Choose what to blur from the popup. Preferences saved instantly.

04

Hover to read

Hover any blurred element to reveal it. Move away and it hides again.
